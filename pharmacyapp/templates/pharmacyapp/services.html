Pharmacy services

The ageing of the population, technological advances, new public health threats, evolving patients' expectations and shrinking budget are putting pressure on health care systems in Europe and beyond. An increasing portion of health care expenditure is on pharmaceuticals and chronic diseases. All these factors are pushing for change. And community pharmacy is changing too to respond to these challenges and meet patients’ needs by shifting the focus from dispensing medicines and diseases to patient care and quality of services. Pharmacy services aim to achieve the ‘triple aim’:

  • Better quality of care
  • Better health outcome
  • Lower costs

Core pharmacy services are:

  • Consultations
  • Minor surgery
  • Outpatient & inpatient care
  • Laboratory
  • Family planning
  • Counseling & guidance
